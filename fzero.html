Without a doubt the best game of the racing genre to me, this speed of sound game hits everything just right, the courses, art direction, gameplay, music, everything adds up to be all around one of my all time favorites. Even if left untouched since this 2003 entry, what is here is enough to keep players around the world trying their best to date to achieve the best records possible. For those interested I recommend the F-Zero Central website for more information.

With that said, I personally put some restrictions on myself in the interest of my personal health, as well as the health of my controllers, certain exploits used by world record holders have had known negative effects, and personally I think take away from the charm of the game largely. While my times are not world record material, I am proud of the times I have achieved, check the details below, and maybe you can compete against me!
