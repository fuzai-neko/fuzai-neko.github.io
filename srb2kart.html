Always a fun time, SRB2 Kart comes from a great community driven project years in the making and still actively developed. SRB2Kart is the definitive Kart Racer to me, a free PC game with loads of mods, solid gameplay, and online multiplayer, truly a dream come true! Check them out here!

Here I have time trials for vanilla courses and some mods I enjoy, links provided where possible for course or character mods. This is more of a loose leaderboard due to the ever changing nature of a live service game such as this.
